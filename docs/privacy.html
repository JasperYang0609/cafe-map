☕ Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

ansai built and operates the BeanGo app. By using our service, you agree to this policy.

1. Data Collection

We collect: location (to show nearby cafes, only while using the app), account info (email/password, encrypted), usage data (search history, favorites), and device info (OS version, device type).

2. Data Usage

Location is only used to search nearby cafes, not for tracking. We do not sell personal data to third parties. Anonymous statistics may be used to improve service quality.

3. Third-Party Services

We use: Google Maps Platform, Supabase (authentication/storage), Google AdMob (ads for free users), and Apple/Google (subscription payments). Each has its own privacy policy.

4. Data Protection

All data transmitted via HTTPS. Passwords stored with industry-standard encryption. We regularly review security measures.

5. Your Rights

You can: delete search history, remove favorites, delete your account and all data, revoke location permissions (in device settings).

6. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified by updating the "Last updated" date.
